FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is LIMS in pathology laboratories?▾
LIMS is software that manages the complete lifecycle of a sample — from patient registration to report delivery. It centralizes sample tracking, result entry, report generation, and compliance documentation in one connected platform.
Is ERBA LIMS suitable for small labs?▾
Yes. The system works for both small single-location labs and large diagnostic chains. It is configured based on your test volume and workflow — not a fixed template. Whether you process 100 or 5,000 tests a day, the system scales with you.
Which analyzers does ERBA LIMS integrate with?▾
ERBA LIMS integrates with Sysmex, Mindray, Beckman Coulter, Roche, Abbott, and other major brands. If your analyzer model is not listed, contact us and we will confirm compatibility directly.
Is ERBA LIMS compliant with NABL and ISO 15189?▾
The system automatically maintains audit trails, QC records, and documentation in line with ISO 15189 requirements — so you are always audit-ready without extra manual effort.
How long does setup take?▾
Most labs are operational within a few days. Setup includes configuring your test panels, departments, and workflows. We provide hands-on training for your entire team.
Can patients receive reports on WhatsApp?▾
Yes. Once a report is approved, it is sent automatically to the patient's WhatsApp number and email — no printing, no waiting for physical pickup.
